## 🐛 Common Issues & Solutions

**Issue: Blur not working**
Solution: Some older browsers don't support backdrop-filter. Fallback colors provided.

**Issue: Firebase errors**
Solution: Complete ALL steps in FIREBASE-SETUP-GUIDE.md

**Issue: Search not working**
Solution: Ensure data has title, content, and category fields

**Issue: Theme not saving**
Solution: Check browser localStorage is enabled
