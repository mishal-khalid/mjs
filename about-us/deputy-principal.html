About

Hawwa Nazima serves as the Deputy Principal of Mohamed Jamaluddin School and is the Academic In-charge of the school. As a member of the Senior Management Team, she works closely with the school's leadership to uphold the institution's academic standards and to support the day-to-day running of teaching and learning across the school.

In her role as Academic In-charge, she helps guide the academic direction of the school — coordinating with teachers, supporting students, and ensuring that the school's programmes are delivered to a high standard in line with Mohamed Jamaluddin School's mission of building excellence.

Areas of Responsibility

  • Overseeing the academic operations and programmes of the school.
  • Supporting and coordinating the teaching staff.
  • Contributing to school leadership as part of the Senior Management Team.
  • Helping maintain the academic standards and discipline of the school.
